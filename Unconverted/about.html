About Us

Our Mission

The mission of Sprout Creek Farm is to educate people to understand and appreciate our connection to the earth and our responsibility for its care. As well as producing and selling our meats and cheeses, the purposes of the farm are:

▶ Provide educational experiences for children and adults that will foster appreciation for, and understanding of, our place within the natural world, and thus encourage responsibility for the future of our environment

▶Offer programs that will reconnect people to the rich agricultural heritage of the United States to offer an experience of community as a model of peaceful living

▶Offer programs in spiritual development, using the agricultural resources of the farm as both setting and starting point of such programs

Our Farm

Sprout Creek Farm is a center for education, farming, community building and spiritual growth. It is a place of work, of peace, of intellectual inquiry and experiential learning. It is home to a community of people, plants and animals; farmland, forest, streams and ponds, wetlands and hedgegrows, hills and rocky soils. We are a productive farm where we make cheese from the milk produced by our cows and goats. Right here we can watch the grass become milk that in turn becomes the cheese enjoyed at our table and yours. We welcome you to take a "bird's eye view" and learn more about the different programs we host, our animals and the land we farm. Better yet, come visit the Farm and experience the peacefulness yourself. 

We are open to visitors everyday from 10 am to 5 pm. 

We hope to see you soon!
