Summer Camp

Give your child an unforgettable summer experience at Sprout Creek Farm.

Encompassing 200 acres in Dutchess County, NY, Sprout Creek Farm is a working dairy production farm, complete with a creamery and education center. We invite children of all ages to come to the farm for day or overnight camp to experience everything Sprout Creek has to offer.

What Do You Do At Sprout Creek?!

Campers will get to know our animals, learn about and participate in the tasks and projects necessary to run the farm, and enjoy the natural beauty of the extraordinary property. All camp counselors are undergraduate students from Marist College in Poughkeepsie. The Marist Fellows studying education, psychology, and other social and behavioral sciences and are interested in working with children and youth.

Summer marks the height of activity at Sprout Creek Farm. Our cows and goats are at peak milk production, our garden is full of vegetables ready to be harvested and the creek is finally warm enough for a dip. Campers will share in the work—and play—that makes summer at Sprout Creek a time to build lasting friendships, embark on rewarding endeavors, and create lifelong memories.
